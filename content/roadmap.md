## Phase 1 — Work Completed

### Research

**[Indirect Prompt Injection in Local LLMs](/research/indirect-prompt-injection-llama3/)** — March 2026

A systematic test of 36 injection attempts against Llama 3.2 3B across three variables: injection placement, injection style, and task type. The model followed embedded attacker instructions in **77.8% of tests** with high confidence. End-of-document placement achieved a 100% injection rate. No meaningful discrimination was observed between injection styles — including forged system authority claims.

Full methodology, raw results, and reproduction instructions are published openly.

### Infrastructure

**Multi-platform content distribution** — Automated publishing pipeline covering YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, Reels, Stories, Bluesky, and Threads from a single workflow. Built to support consistent, high-frequency output without manual overhead on every platform.

**Bluesky engagement system** — An LLM-powered engagement tool that identifies relevant posts across trending and AI/tech-specific feeds, generates contextual replies tuned to a defined voice, and operates on a natural posting cadence with per-author cooldowns and content filtering. Designed to build genuine presence at scale.

### Research (continued)

**[Contextual Alignment: The Missing Layer in AI-Assisted Development](/research/contextual-alignment/)** — April 2026

A framework for building persistent, structured context that keeps AI collaborators aligned with human intent across sessions. Developed over 21 sessions of building a 57,000+ line game in Godot with Claude Code. Introduces three layers: structural context (auto-generated codebase summaries), preference context (persistent memory and feedback loops), and temporal context (session state management). Includes a case study on teaching AI aesthetic taste through A/B testing and random forest regression.
