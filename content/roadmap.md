**Content strategy:**
- **AI red-teaming** — publicly test frontier models, document failure modes with full methodology
- **AI safety education** — explain alignment, interpretability, Constitutional AI from a practitioner's perspective
- **Leadership & governance** — what AI leadership should actually look like, operationally
